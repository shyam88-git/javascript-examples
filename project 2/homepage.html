Our Mission

The mission of the SHARMA COMPUTER ACADEMY is to provide its students with the foundation for lifelong learning. Whether they choose to continue their education or enter the workforce, SCA students are trained to succeed. Here, at Bhopal, we believe in creating a dynamic learning environment, both inside and outside of the classroom.

About

We at SCA are a group of highly skilled and qualified software professionals, who are working towards to attain mutual goal, the goal of “Moulding The Talented into Most Demanded”. SCA is a premier organization founded in 1998, growing as an IT Training/service providers and determined to set high standards in IT industry.
